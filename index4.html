The Royal Family

The royal family is composed of 3 members, The King Aeeres, and his wife nommed Queen Idyia, and their daughter, Princess Medea. They are protected by the goddess Hera. According to a variety of sources of the legend, The King holds the golden fleece forged with the help of Athena.
